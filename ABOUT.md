# About Buitenkans

Buitenkans is a Dutch project about strengthening pathways into school leadership in primary
education, with specific focus on side-entry (`zij-instroom`) and progression pathways
(`doorstroom`).

This repository is the public-facing digital handreiking (guidance site) for that project.

## What This App Does

- publishes practical insights and recommendations
- shares background resources and frequently asked questions
- highlights contributors and partner organizations
- supports editorial updates through Nuxt Studio

## Project Context

The initiative is connected to Onderwijsregio Oost-Nederland and Stichting Onderwijs in. The goal is
to help school boards, training partners, and schools design realistic and sustainable routes for
future school leaders.
